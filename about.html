About us

Webfala Digital Skills For All Initiative is a non-profit organization established in 2018 with the mission to empower underprivileged children, girls, youths and women in Nigeria(53% of whom live under the poverty line) with STEM education and digital literacy skills that will enable them create their own future and compete favourably in this digital age.

To this end, we provide them with the tools to operate at the speed of digital, to innovate and to ignite social change in their respective communities and beyond. At Webfala, we don't just close the digital gap, we influence and empower marginalised young Nigerians to create a whole new reality and future for themselves. An equal whole wolrd of creativity we unleashed. Our organizations focus is consistent with Sustainable Development Goal(SDG) 4 on Education, SDG 5 on gender equality and SDG 8 on decent work and economic growth.
