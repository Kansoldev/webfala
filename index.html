Webfala Digital Skills For All Initiative

Webfala Digital Skills For All Initiative is a non-profit organization established in 2018 with the mission to empower underprivileged children, girls, youths and women in Nigeria(53% of whom live under the poverty line) with STEM education and digital literacy skills that will enable them create their own future and compete favourably in this digital age
